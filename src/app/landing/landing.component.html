All in one platform, no multiple accounts, complete service for free

Applyfree is a free and all in one platform for finding, being assisted for, applied to and get admitted to your desired higher education choice and to finish it successfully free of any additional charge other than schools’ charges.

Technology in help of efficiency

Applyfree uses advanced search and ranking technology to make all this process as most efficient as possible. Our applyscore™ algorithm predicts the chance of success for every student-school pair and helps you select programs and schools that have been doing better in students’ success.

Step by Step human help

Although we use advanced technology to maximize the chance of student’s success, we believe that one-on-one human help is required for every student to make it till the end. Therefore, applyfree LLC is committed to provide this help by hiring a caring and knowledgeable advisor for every student. For those students who already have an advisor with whom they like to continue, we provide technological help and financial incentives to make the existing relationship persistent and more efficient.

Work with applyfree

Join us if you believe in our mission

We believe in the power of people. Regardless of how powerful a computer algorithm can be, a human being has unique and irreplaceable characteristics with which the algorithm works better. Therefore, we’re not going to move forward with the help of technology alone. We need partners to come along students and applyfree in this long journey. Failure in finishing an education program has measurable and unmeasurable loss for students and their family. Applyfree asks help from every individual or company who is working in the industry as well as those who think they owe their successful career and life to education. We ask your help if you are a caring parent, a school advisor, a teacher, a tutor, a successful student or a registered recruiter to become part of our team.

Create wealth by collaboration

We are not a charity by the way. We charge schools for the service and we compensate every help that is proved to be effective. Our goal is efficient education and we praise who is committed to this purpose by the wealth that will be created by that efficiency.

Long term benefits and Students first approach

We are committed to make the process as less risky as possible for students. That’s why we provide the entire service for them for free. However, as the student moves forward and approaches success, all of us will consequently benefit from that success. The process is simple and has been designed solely based on students’ success.
