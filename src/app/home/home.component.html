Free education for students

applyfree helps students and their parents find meaningful information beneficial to their education path. Before applyfree, each student had to do extensive research and in most cases a purposeless loop of googling with a lot of irrelevant - and in some cases wrong - information awaiting them. Since applyfree, the whole research process has turned into a meaningful search of available "contracts" that predict real meaningful actions. But who provides these contracts? applyfree's search engine is fueled by offerings from one of these groups: 1. school admins, 2. student recruiters and consultants, 3. tutors and private teachers or 4. translators from anywhere in the world.

How applyfree is being used?

applyfree is being used to search for education specific offerings. On the other hand, it is being used to create and publish searchable offerings as contracts. You can enter any contract as the second party when you find it. After finding the right contract for you, you're free to contact the first party in order to negotiate, bargain and communicate whatever you like. At the end, if the first party, the user who has created the contract, agrees to work with you, you'll have a deal.
