About applyfree

Applyfree LLC is a California based technology company working in the education industry. Our mission is to use information technology to make education work around students’ success.

Applyfree uses a platform software architecture to make its service reach as far as it may go with help of its partners. As we grow, which means more students experiencing a more successful education, everybody including students, parents, schools, and our partners will be happier.

Our edurank™ is the core ranking technology we use in our search engine. It can rank schools, school departments, advisors and many more educational entities based on their performance in helping students succeed. We continuously work with highly ranked entities to unravel their methods in keeping their high rank and work with lower ranking entities to help them compete for higher rankings. All these works help nothing but increase the likelihood of students’ success.

Our media team is constantly creating artistic stories from fact-based success. Stories of continuing success as well as the stories of rising from failure. We tell inspiring stories on how good intent along with good work can take away failure and replace it with success.

Our advisors make us more human. They listen to students and help them whenever they feel they need help from applying to graduating.
